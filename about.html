About Me

From missing two teeth to tennis state champion, what is there to know about Jake?

I was born in American Fork, Utah to Liz and David Brown. Shortly after my birth we moved from Provo, Utah to Baltimore, Maryland while my dad pursued his doctorate degree in Dental Surgery. After completing school, we moved to the great plains of Lawrence, Kansas where we lived until I was nearly 11. Since then we have lived in Southern Utah in St. George. My father is a practicing dentist while my mother helps my three siblings and I survive day to day.

I have enjoyed a very active lifestyle. My family enjoys hiking, playing sports, camping, and more. Our favorite indoor pasttimes include board games and movies. I am an avid movie watcher and enjoy them probably more than the average Joe.

A quick list of things I enjoy include: Piano, tennis, soccer, basketball, working out, listening to music, reading, and more. I have never been one to enjoy boredom and have sought to fill my available time to the brim and sometimes overflowing. Shoutout to my mother for driving me to all my practices and performances so I could do all the things I enjoyed.
